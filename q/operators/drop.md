# `_` drop

**References:**
- https://code.kx.com/q/ref/drop/

Drop items from a list, entries from a dictionary or columns from a table.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Syntax

~~~~
x _ y
~~~~

~~~~
_[x; y]
~~~~

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



**References:**
- https://code.kx.com/q/ref/drop/#drop-leading-or-trailing-items



### Examples

~~~~
5 _ 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 
~~~~
